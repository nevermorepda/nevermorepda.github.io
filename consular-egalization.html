COnsular legalization overview

Consular Legalization is a procedure legalizing documents and certifying the authenticity of the seal, signature on the documents that was issued in one country to have legal use in another country.

Eligibility (Who can apply for consular legalization?)

This procedure is required when a foreign national wants to use the original documents issued in his or her own country to have legal use in Vietnam and likewise for Vietnamese citizens. Understanding the complexity of the paperwork process in Vietnam, we aim to become the solution to your Vietnam's legal document requests. No longer have to feel frustrated by the strenuous paperwork procedures required by the authorities, we are here to ease your burden and get things done efficiently on your behalf.
