Overview and Eligibility

TOURIST EVISA

Tourism E-Visa (30 days - single entry) is an official travel document issued electronically that permits foreigners to enter and stay within a foreign country. Foreigners traveling to Vietnam as a tourist needs to have a Tourism Visa in addition with valid passport as the required travel documents to legally enter the country.

business eVisa

Business E-Visa (30 days - single entry) is an official travel document issued electronically that permits foreigners to enter and stay within a foreign country. Foreigners traveling to Vietnam as a Businessmen/Businesswomen needs to have a Business Visa in addition with valid passport as the required travel documents to legally enter the country.
