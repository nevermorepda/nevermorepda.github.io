Vietnam passport Overview

Passport is a government-owned document that the Ministry of Public Security issued to Vietnamese residents for entry and exit, for the purpose of nationality and identity verification. The validity period of a passport is no more than 10 years for citizens 14 years of age and older, and less than 5 years for citizens under 14 years of age.
