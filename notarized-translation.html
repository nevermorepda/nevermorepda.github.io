Notarized translation Overview

Notarized Translation is a process in which a document is translated into another language and then certified by a notary public. This ensures that the translation is accurate and that the translator is qualified to translate the document.
Notarized Translation is an important service that can help you to ensure that your documents are accurate and that they will be accepted by the authorities. If you need to have a document translated into Vietnamese, you should consider using a notarized translation service.

Eligibility (Who need notarized translation service?)

This procedure is required when a foreign national wants to use the original documents issued in his or her own country to have legal use in Vietnam by translating and notarizing the documents to prove its authenticity. Detailed advice is always available by contacting or applying with us.
