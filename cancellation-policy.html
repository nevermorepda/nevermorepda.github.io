Visa On Arrival ( VOA )

Processing time

Tourist Visa :
+ Normal case: 7-10 working day (excluding Holidays, weekend)
+ Urgent case: 3-5 working day(excluding Holidays, weekend )

Business Visa :
+ Normal case: 7-12 working day (excluding Holidays, weekend)

The processing time depends on the type of visa, nationality and type of processing that you applied for your Vietnam visa (process visa in normal case, urgent case, emergency case).

When we receive your payment, we will send you a confirmation via Email. This Email will inform you of a payment confirmation and a "Processing Notification". 

A "Processing Notification" will show that the visa application will be processed on [date] . We will send you a result on [date] at [time].

The processing time is dependent on business hours, which are 8:00 AM to 16:00 PM (GMT+7) Monday to Friday (excluding lunch time from 11:00 AM to 13:30 PM); and is also subject to Vietnam National Public Holidays.
 
Working time will be based on Vietnam Time (GMT+7).
The processing time is based on the applicant acceptance time and processing time of the Vietnam Immigration (Vietnam Time), and the following rules will be applied:

o   For normal cases (7-10 or 7-12 working day): If we receive your payment and all required information before [11:00 am] of the working day, your processing time will be counted immediately, and your Visa On Arrival will be ready by the end of the 10th (for Tourist visa) or 12th working day (for business visa) at the latest. Otherwise, it will be counted on the next working day.

o   For urgent cases, the time receiving your visa will be confirmed case by case.
The issuing authority may reject any visa, passport or other travel document application for any reasons and may not provide a reason to [web] for the rejection. We have no responsibility for any delays, cancellations, financial and other losses due to denial or delay in processing. 

Prior to approval, the issuing authority may ask for additional documentations and we will inform you by Email to prepare a full set of required documents. Since we do not issue visas or passports and cannot make any guarantee or assurances that any issuing authority will issue any documents nor can we guarantee the time required for an issuing authority to grant or reject an application. Issuing authorities may choose to grant a different type of travel document than that which the applicant has requested which may impact the purpose, validity or number of entries. Tickets or reservations that are non-refundable should not be made until all necessary travel documents for your trip have been obtained.

We will send you an email with the subject "Application #ID: Visa for Vietnam Remind (gate OnePay) - [Name of applicant]" " if your payment is failed and instructs you to try again using a different payment method. Any problems that result from a failed payment are not our responsibility. Stamping fee :
The stamping fee is charged by the Vietnam Immigration Department. Cash must be used to pay this charge at the airport. USD is strongly advised to avoid a high exchange rate.
You will not be required to pay this charge again if you pay it online with us
The stamping fee varies depending on your visa type (single or multiple entry).
In any case, the stamping fee is not refundable.
