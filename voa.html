Overview and Eligibility

tourist visa on arrival

Visa On Arrival is an official Visa to travel to Vietnam issued for visitors whose nationals are not in the 80 countries eligible for E-Visa. It is also the Visa used for business travelers who need a longer stay in Vietnam. Visa On Arrival is a much more convenient & time saving option for visitors as it is applied and processed electronically as opposed to manual application at Vietnam Embassy/Consulate.

Validity period: 15 days - single entry
*Note: This type of Visa will be processed based on your return air ticket. Therefore, kindly book a flight around 7 - 12 working days in advance.

See all 80 eligible countries for eVisa
business visa on arrival

A Vietnam Long-term Business Visa (also known as a DN visa) is a type of visa that allows foreigners to enter and remain in Vietnam for a period of 3 months for the purposes of working with Vietnamese enterprises or conducting business activities such as meetings, signing business contracts, etc.

Long-term Business Visa can get at the airport or the Vietnam Embassy nearby your location.

Validity period: 03 months - multiple entries
*Note: To get Business Visa On Arrival, you must have a sponsor company in Vietnam to finish the documents.
