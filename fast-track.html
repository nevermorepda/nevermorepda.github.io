fAST tRACK Overview

Fast Track is an airport priority service that allow passengers expedited through all the airport procedures without staying in long queue to get line and wait for the endless line at the Airport.

Arrival Fast track

Meet & Greet at arrival airport
Our staff will wait with the name board to meet you at the Landing Visa & Immigration Area (where allowed).

Visa & Immigration Procedure and Security clearance
Once you land, our staff will assist you with the flight procedures such as acquiring a Visa, getting through passport control without waiting in a long queue, customs and security clearance.

Porter service
Our staff collect your luggage at the baggage claim area and bring it to your vehicle.

Departure fast track

Meet & Greet at departure airport
Our staff will wait with the name board to meet you at the curb
Note: Meeting points vary according to specific airports

Porter service
It includes collecting luggage from your vehicle to the check-in point.

Check-in assistance
Our staff will assist you during the check-in procedure at check-in counter without having to waiting in airport lines.

Get through the passport & customs counter
Our staff will assist you to get through the passport and customs counter without standing in a long queue.
