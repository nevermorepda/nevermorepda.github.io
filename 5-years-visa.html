5 years Visa Exemption Overview

Vietnamese people residing overseas who have passports or laissez-passers issued by foreign authorities, foreigners being their spouses or children; foreigners being spouses and children of Vietnamese citizens shall be granted visa-free entry as prescribed by the government. 5-Years Visa Exemption Certificate shall be issued to people entering Vietnam for family visit and settlement of personal business. 5-Years Visa Exemption Certificate is valid up to 05 years and expires at least 06 months before the expiry of the applicant’s passport. Duration of stay does not exceed 180 days for each entry.

+ Vietnamese residing abroad who still has Vietnamese birth certificate
